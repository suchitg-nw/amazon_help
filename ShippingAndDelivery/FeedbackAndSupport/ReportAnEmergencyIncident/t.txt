Report an Emergency Incident
If you witness or experience a critical safety emergency incident involving Shipped by Amazon delivery agents, you can report it to emergency services and/or us.

To report an emergency incident:
Make sure you're safe.
Call 112 for emergency services, if needed.
Contact us for help. We're available 24/7.
For help in English, Hindi, Telugu, and Bengali: Call 844-311-0406.
For help in Kannada or Tamil: Contact us at https://www.amazon.in/contact-us.
