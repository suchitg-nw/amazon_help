
Appeal a Denied A-to-z Guarantee Refund

If we deny your A-to-z Guarantee Refund request for purchases made on Amazon.in, you can appeal the decision.

To make an appeal for purchases made on Amazon.in:

    Go to Your Orders and select View Order Details.
    Locate the order.
    Select Problem with order.
    Select Appeal decision.
    Enter your comments in the text box.
    Select Submit.

Note: You can appeal a denied request within 30 calendar days of receiving notification of the denial. We'll review denied requests and notify you of the final decision within three business days.
